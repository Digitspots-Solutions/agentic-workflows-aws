Executive Summary
Ladoke Akintola University of Technology (LAUTECH) is a premier Nigerian technical university serving over 10,000 students. They are responsible for housing, financial clearance, and general welfare. The volume of students relative to the administrative staff count has historically created service bottlenecks.
